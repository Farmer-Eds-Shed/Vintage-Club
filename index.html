About the Club

Meath Vintage Club, is a group of like minded owners and enthusiasts of vintage and classic vehicles and machinery. We have regular meetups to discuss recent sales, purchases and workshop experiences. Everyone is welcome to join whether you are an owner, looking for your first project or just an enthusiast.
